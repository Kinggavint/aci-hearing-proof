Starkey Genesis AI Hearing Aids in Lafayette, LA

ACI Hearing Center fits the full Starkey lineup as an independent practice in Lafayette, Louisiana. We do not have a manufacturer contract with Starkey or any other brand. We recommend Starkey Genesis AI when the audiogram, ear anatomy, and lifestyle add up to it. This page explains what makes Genesis AI different, which patients benefit most from it, and what to expect from a Starkey fit at ACI.

Why Starkey Genesis AI leads for health tracking

Starkey is the only major American hearing aid manufacturer. Starkey Hearing Technologies is headquartered in Eden Prairie, Minnesota, where Genesis AI is designed and assembled. Every other major brand (Oticon, Phonak, ReSound, Widex, Signia) is manufactured in Denmark, Switzerland, or Singapore. That fact matters to some patients and not to others, but it is a real differentiator.

What really sets Genesis AI apart is the health-and-safety feature set. Genesis AI is the only major hearing aid on the market with built-in fall detection, heart rate monitoring, and step tracking. It uses an accelerometer similar to what you find in an Apple Watch. If it detects the motion pattern of a fall, it automatically sends an alert to up to three contacts you set up in the My Starkey app. You can also manually trigger an alert by tapping the hearing aid three times.

This is not a replacement for a medical alert system like Life Alert or a smartwatch, but it is a real safety net built into a device most patients already wear from morning until bedtime. For older adults living alone, patients with balance issues, and families who worry about a parent falling, Genesis AI gives peace of mind without asking the patient to wear an extra device.

The trade-off: Genesis AI is not the most aggressive on noise suppression in loud restaurants (that crown belongs to Phonak Audeo Sphere Infinio) and it does not yet support Auracast public broadcast (that crown belongs to ReSound Nexia). If restaurants are your biggest complaint, we steer you to Phonak. If public streaming at airports and church PA systems is your priority, we steer you to Nexia. If health tracking, fall detection, or American-made hearing aids are what you value most, Genesis AI is our pick.

Current Starkey models we fit

Starkey Genesis AI (adult, first-line)

Genesis AI is a receiver-in-canal device and our most-fit Starkey model. Rechargeable, about 24 hours per charge without streaming. iPhone Made for iPhone with hands-free calling and Siri integration. Android ASHA for direct streaming. Includes fall detection, heart rate monitoring, step tracking, and Multiflex Tinnitus Technology for patients with ringing in the ears.

What we like: Genesis AI's health-and-safety feature set is real and works well out of the box. The My Starkey app is well-designed and older patients who struggle with tech usually manage it. Setup takes about 10 minutes in-office to configure the fall-detection contacts. What to know: Genesis AI's speech-in-noise performance is very good but not the class leader. Multiflex Tinnitus Technology is one of the best masking options in the industry, but for the strongest tinnitus relief we may combine it with Lenire treatment.

Starkey Livio Edge AI (previous generation, still-supported)

Livio Edge AI was the Starkey flagship before Genesis AI and remains available for existing Livio patients who need replacements or upgrades. Livio Edge AI has an earlier version of the health-tracking feature set (fall detection and step count, but not the full heart rate monitoring in Genesis AI). We do not first-fit Livio Edge AI anymore. If you already wear Livio Edge AI and are happy, we service them. If you are new to Starkey, we go straight to Genesis AI.

Starkey Livio (entry-level Starkey lineup)

Livio is Starkey's older entry-level lineup, still available for patients on a strict budget who want Starkey's core sound quality and American assembly without the full health-tracking feature set. Livio can be a good fit for patients who prefer disposable batteries or who do not want smartphone integration. We fit Livio with full real-ear measurement verification just like every other device.

How a Starkey fit works at ACI

  1. Full audiological workup. Air conduction, bone conduction, speech-in-noise, tympanometry, and if indicated, otoacoustic emissions. This tells us your loss shape and how much room there is for improvement with amplification.
  2. Discussion and recommendation. We walk you through the top 2-3 brands that fit your audiogram, lifestyle, and budget. Starkey Genesis AI is often on that list, especially if fall detection, heart rate monitoring, or American-made hearing aids matter to you. If a different brand fits better we say so.
  3. Prescriptive fit with real-ear measurement. We do not just plug in the manufacturer's first-fit software. We use a probe microphone in your ear canal to measure the actual output at your eardrum and match it to a validated prescription (NAL-NL2 for adults). Only about 30% of dispensers actually do this. Every ACI fit gets REM verification.
  4. Health-feature setup. If you pick Genesis AI, we spend about 15 minutes setting up the My Starkey app on your phone, adding fall-detection contacts, and showing you how to check step count and heart rate. Family members are welcome to come in for this step.
  5. 45-day trial and follow-up fine-tuning. You wear the devices in your real life. We fine-tune based on what you notice. Included in the fee.
  6. 3-year manufacturer warranty and 3-year loss-and-damage coverage on premium tiers.

Frequently asked questions about Starkey at ACI

Does ACI Hearing Center fit Starkey hearing aids in Lafayette, LA?

Yes. Starkey is one of the six major hearing aid brands we fit as an independent practice. We fit the full current lineup: Genesis AI (adult first-line, with health tracking), Livio Edge AI (still-supported previous generation for existing patients), and Livio (entry-level). All fits are verified with real-ear measurement.

What makes Starkey Genesis AI different from other hearing aids?

Two things. First, Genesis AI is the only major hearing aid brand with built-in fall detection, heart rate monitoring, and step tracking. The Thrive Care app can automatically alert up to three family members if a fall is detected. That matters for older adults living alone, patients with balance issues, and family members who worry. Second, Starkey is American-designed and assembled at their Eden Prairie, Minnesota facility. Every other major hearing aid brand is manufactured in Denmark, Switzerland, or Singapore.

How does Starkey Genesis AI compare to Phonak Audeo Sphere Infinio?

Both are premium receiver-in-canal hearing aids. Phonak leads on aggressive AI noise suppression for very noisy environments and hands-free phone calls. Starkey Genesis AI leads on health tracking (the only major brand with fall detection and heart rate). Patients whose main frustration is restaurants and noisy family dinners usually prefer Phonak. Patients who also want a health-and-safety wearable built into their hearing aid, or who prefer American-made, usually prefer Starkey.

Does Starkey Genesis AI work with iPhone and Android?

Yes. Genesis AI works with iPhone via Apple's Made for iPhone protocol with hands-free calling and Siri integration. On Android, it uses Android ASHA for direct streaming. The My Starkey app runs on both iPhone and Android to control programs, adjust volume, and view step count, heart rate, and activity data.

How does the fall detection work?

Genesis AI has a built-in accelerometer, similar to the sensor in an Apple Watch. If it detects the sudden motion pattern of a fall (a rapid change in position followed by lack of movement), it will automatically send an alert to up to three contacts you set in the My Starkey app. You can also manually trigger an alert by tapping the hearing aid three times. This is not a replacement for a medical alert system but it is a real safety net built into a device most patients already wear all day.

Are Starkey hearing aids rechargeable?

Yes. Genesis AI is rechargeable and gives about 24 hours per charge without streaming. Livio Edge AI is available in both rechargeable and disposable-battery versions. Livio is available with disposable battery. The rechargeable case doubles as a portable charger for travel.

How much do Starkey hearing aids cost at ACI Hearing Center?

At ACI Hearing Center, we price complete treatment plans, not just the Starkey device. Treatment plans that include Starkey Genesis AI, Livio Edge AI, or Livio binaural hearing aids range from $4,500 to $7,500 and include a real-ear measurement fitting, a 45-day trial, follow-up adjustments, and a 3-year manufacturer warranty. Every plan qualifies for 0% financing through Cherry, CareCredit, or PowerPay for qualified applicants.

Is Starkey Genesis AI good for tinnitus?

Yes. Starkey has been a leader in tinnitus masking for a long time. Genesis AI includes Multiflex Tinnitus Technology, which generates customizable sound-therapy signals inside the hearing aid to give relief from ringing. If tinnitus is your main concern, we can pair Starkey with our full tinnitus protocol including Lenire bimodal neuromodulation and tinnitus retraining therapy where indicated.

Does Starkey work with the Auracast public broadcast standard?

Genesis AI does not currently support Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast. That is where ReSound Nexia leads. If public broadcast streaming (airports, church PA systems, gyms) is your top priority, we would steer you to Nexia. If health tracking and safety features are your top priority, Genesis AI is our pick.
