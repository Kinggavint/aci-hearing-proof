About Dr. Sarah Lalande-Davies

Dr. Sarah Lalande-Davies, Au.D., is a Doctor of Audiology at ACI Hearing Center in Lafayette, Louisiana. She joined the practice in November 2020 and has been serving Acadiana patients ever since.

Dr. Lalande-Davies is a Baton Rouge native. She earned her Bachelor of Science in Communication Sciences and Disorders from Louisiana State University and her Doctor of Audiology (Au.D.) from LSU Health Sciences Center in New Orleans. She completed her fourth-year externship at the Southeast Louisiana Veterans Health Care System, where she had the honor of serving our nation's veterans.

Dr. Lalande-Davies is passionate about helping patients reconnect with the sounds that matter most. She finds great joy in seeing the impact improved hearing has on daily life, and enjoys getting to know each patient along the way.

When she's not in the clinic, Dr. Lalande-Davies enjoys spending time with her husband, son, and dog, especially going for walks together and visiting the local farmers market. She and her family are LSU football season ticket holders and love cheering on the Tigers. She also enjoys traveling, reading, listening to music, and spending time with family and friends.

Areas of specialty

  • Comprehensive diagnostic hearing evaluations
  • Hearing aid fitting and programming
  • Tinnitus management and counseling
  • Cochlear implant support and mapping
  • Real-ear measurement verified hearing aid fittings

Education and training

Dr. Lalande-Davies earned her Bachelor of Science in Communication Sciences and Disorders at Louisiana State University and her Doctor of Audiology (Au.D.) at LSU Health Sciences Center in New Orleans. She completed her fourth-year externship at the Southeast Louisiana Veterans Health Care System, serving veterans with complex hearing care needs. She joined ACI Hearing Center in November 2020 and holds her Certificate of Clinical Competence in Audiology (CCC-A) through the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association.

Services Dr. Sarah Lalande-Davies provides

Dr. Sarah Lalande-Davies provides the full range of audiology care at ACI Hearing Center. Learn more about any of these services:

  • Hearing Tests — comprehensive diagnostic hearing evaluations
  • Hearing Aids — modern digital hearing aids with real-ear verified fittings
  • Tinnitus Management — evaluation and personalized care plans
  • Cochlear Implants — candidacy evaluation, mapping, and follow-up
  • Real-Ear Measurement — objective verification of hearing aid programming
  • Pediatric Audiology — hearing care for infants, children, and teens

Frequently asked questions

When did Dr. Sarah Lalande-Davies join ACI Hearing Center?

Dr. Lalande-Davies joined ACI Hearing Center in November 2020, after completing her Doctor of Audiology degree at LSU Health Sciences Center in New Orleans and her fourth-year externship at the Southeast Louisiana Veterans Health Care System.

What are Dr. Lalande-Davies's specialties?

Dr. Lalande-Davies provides comprehensive diagnostic hearing evaluations, hearing aid fitting, tinnitus management, and cochlear implant care for patients across the lifespan. She has strong clinical experience from her VA externship serving veterans with complex hearing needs.

Where did Dr. Lalande-Davies go to school?

Dr. Lalande-Davies earned her Bachelor of Science in Communication Sciences and Disorders from Louisiana State University, then her Doctor of Audiology from LSU Health Sciences Center in New Orleans. Her fourth-year externship was at the Southeast Louisiana Veterans Health Care System.

Does Dr. Lalande-Davies see pediatric patients?

Yes. Dr. Lalande-Davies sees patients of all ages. She provides diagnostic testing and hearing aid care for children, adults, and seniors.

How do I book an appointment with Dr. Lalande-Davies?

Call ACI Hearing Center at 337-223-9448 and let our team know you would like to see Dr. Lalande-Davies. We will schedule you at a time that works for both of you.

Is Dr. Lalande-Davies licensed in Louisiana?

Yes. Dr. Lalande-Davies is licensed by the Louisiana Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology and holds her Certificate of Clinical Competence in Audiology (CCC-A) through the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association.
