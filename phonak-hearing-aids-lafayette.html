Phonak Hearing Aids in Lafayette, LA

ACI Hearing Center fits the full Phonak lineup as an independent practice in Lafayette, Louisiana. We do not have a manufacturer contract with Phonak or any other brand. We recommend Phonak when the audiogram, ear anatomy, and lifestyle add up to it. This page explains how Phonak fits into the current market, which models we fit and why, and what to expect from a Phonak fit at ACI.

Why Phonak leads for noise environments and phone calls

Phonak is one of the two largest hearing aid manufacturers in the world, owned by Sonova (based in Switzerland). What sets Phonak apart is aggressive AI-driven noise suppression. The current flagship, Audeo Sphere Infinio, uses a dedicated chip called DEEPSONIC that runs a neural network trained on 22 million real-world sound samples. It separates speech from background noise in real time, and it does so more aggressively than any competitor on the market today.

This matters for patients whose top complaint is speech understanding in restaurants, family gatherings, industrial sites, or large classrooms. If aggressive noise reduction is what you want, Phonak delivers it better than anyone.

The other Phonak advantage is Bluetooth. Phonak was the first brand to support universal Bluetooth (both iPhone and Android streaming and hands-free calling without a special app), and they still have the strongest cross-platform performance. If you switch between phone platforms or want true hands-free phone calls, Phonak is a natural fit.

The trade-off: aggressive noise reduction can also strip out ambient sound cues the brain uses to locate speakers or feel present in a space. Patients who want a more natural, immersive listening experience often prefer Oticon Real instead. We help you pick based on your daily life, not on marketing.

Current Phonak models we fit

Phonak Audeo Sphere Infinio (adult, first-line)

Audeo Sphere Infinio is a receiver-in-canal device and our most-fit Phonak model. Rechargeable-only, about 16 hours per charge with streaming. Universal Bluetooth (iPhone and Android) for both streaming and hands-free calls. DEEPSONIC AI chip for noise suppression. Pairs with up to eight devices at once, so patients can flip between phone, laptop, tablet, and TV without unpairing.

What we like: for patients whose main frustration is restaurants, family dinners, or noisy workplaces, Audeo Sphere Infinio consistently outperforms competitors in blinded listening tests. What to know: the DEEPSONIC noise processing can make the world sound "cleaner" but also less natural. Some patients love this. Some do not. The 45-day trial is where you find out.

Phonak Naida (severe to profound, super-power BTE)

Phonak Naida is a super-power behind-the-ear device and one of the strongest hearing aids on the market. Our default recommendation for adults with severe to profound loss when a receiver-in-canal device does not have enough headroom. Available with rechargeable or disposable battery, custom earmolds standard. Naida supports the Roger remote-microphone system and pairs with Phonak's TV Connector and Partner Mic for streaming from external sources.

What we like: for adults on the edge of cochlear implant candidacy, Naida is often the last hearing aid we try before we discuss implant evaluation.

Phonak Sky (pediatric with Roger)

Phonak Sky is designed for children age 5 and up. It is the pediatric sibling of Audeo Sphere Infinio and shares the Bluetooth + Roger-compatible platform. Sky supports the Roger classroom microphone system, which places a wireless mic on a teacher or coach and streams their voice directly into the child's hearing aids. Roger has strong published outcomes for kids with hearing loss in mainstream classrooms. Sky is available with rechargeable or tamper-resistant disposable battery and comes with pediatric earmold options.

What we like: Roger + Sky is the standard-of-care for school-age children in Louisiana, and we coordinate directly with Individualized Education Programs and school audiologists when needed.

How a Phonak fit works at ACI

  1. Full audiological workup. Air conduction, bone conduction, speech-in-noise, tympanometry, and if indicated, otoacoustic emissions. This tells us your loss shape and how much room there is for improvement with amplification.
  2. Discussion and recommendation. We walk you through the top 2-3 brands that fit your audiogram, lifestyle, and budget. Phonak is often on that list, especially if you spend meaningful time in noisy environments or want strong hands-free calls. If a different brand fits better we say so.
  3. Prescriptive fit with real-ear measurement. We do not just plug in the manufacturer's first-fit software. We use a probe microphone in your ear canal to measure the actual output at your eardrum and match it to a validated prescription (NAL-NL2 for adults, DSL for kids). Only about 30% of dispensers actually do this. Every ACI fit gets REM verification.
  4. 45-day trial and follow-up fine-tuning. You wear the devices in your real life. We fine-tune based on what you notice. Included in the fee.
  5. 3-year manufacturer warranty and 3-year loss-and-damage coverage on premium tiers.

Frequently asked questions about Phonak at ACI

Does ACI Hearing Center fit Phonak hearing aids in Lafayette, LA?

Yes. Phonak is one of the two largest hearing aid brands in the world and has been on our first-line list for over a decade. We fit the full current Phonak lineup: Audeo Sphere Infinio for most adults, Naida for severe-to-profound loss, and Sky for children age 5 and up with the Roger classroom system.

What is DEEPSONIC and why does Phonak build around it?

DEEPSONIC is a dedicated AI noise-suppression chip inside Phonak Audeo Sphere Infinio. It runs a neural network trained on 22 million real-world sound samples to separate speech from noise in real time. This is more aggressive than most competitors and it is why Phonak dominates for patients whose top priority is speech understanding in loud environments.

How does Phonak Audeo Sphere Infinio compare to Oticon Real?

Both are premium receiver-in-canal hearing aids. Phonak uses aggressive AI noise suppression to strip background sound and leave speech alone. Oticon Real preserves the full 360-degree sound scene and lets the brain do the sorting. Patients whose main frustration is restaurants and family dinners usually prefer Phonak. Patients whose priority is a natural-sounding, immersive listening experience usually prefer Oticon. We help you pick based on your actual daily life.

Does Phonak Audeo Sphere Infinio work with iPhone and Android?

Yes. Phonak has the strongest cross-platform Bluetooth in the industry. Audeo Sphere Infinio uses universal Bluetooth Classic and Bluetooth LE Audio to stream from any iPhone or Android phone, and it supports true hands-free calling on both platforms without a special app. It also pairs with up to eight devices simultaneously.

Are Phonak hearing aids rechargeable?

Yes. Audeo Sphere Infinio is rechargeable-only and gives about 16 hours per charge with streaming. Naida is available with rechargeable or disposable battery. Sky (pediatric) is available with rechargeable or tamper-resistant disposable battery.

How much do Phonak hearing aids cost at ACI Hearing Center?

Phonak Audeo Sphere Infinio ranges from about $2,600 to $7,400 per pair depending on technology tier. Naida and Sky price similarly. All fits include real-ear measurement verification, follow-up fine-tuning, a 45-day trial, and 3-year manufacturer warranty. We also offer 0% financing through Cherry.

Do you fit Phonak Sky for children and the Roger classroom system?

Yes. Phonak Sky is designed for children age 5 and up and pairs with the Roger remote-microphone system that improves classroom listening. Roger technology has strong published outcomes for kids with hearing loss in mainstream classrooms. We fit Sky with custom earmolds, tamper-resistant options, and full real-ear measurement verification.

Does Phonak Naida still lead for severe-to-profound hearing loss?

Yes. Phonak Naida is a super-power behind-the-ear device and one of the strongest options in the market alongside Oticon Xceed. Naida supports the Roger system, custom earmolds, and both rechargeable and disposable battery. For adults on the edge of cochlear implant candidacy, Naida is often the last hearing aid we try before we discuss implant evaluation.
