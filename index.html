Our Services

Comprehensive Hearing Care

From your first hearing evaluation to ongoing care and adjustments, we provide complete audiology services for patients of all ages.

Hearing Tests

Comprehensive evaluations including speech audiometry, tympanometry, and auditory brainstem response testing.

Learn more

Hearing Aids

Custom-fit hearing aids with real-ear measurements from top brands including Widex, Starkey, Phonak, and more.

Learn more

Tinnitus Management

Certified Lenire provider offering FDA-approved treatment. Sound therapy, maskers, and personalized relief strategies.

Learn more

Cochlear Implants

Complete evaluation and candidacy assessment for cochlear implants, including Cochlear Nucleus devices.

Learn more

Hearing Aid Repair

Expert repair and maintenance to keep your hearing aids performing at their best.

Learn more

Balance Testing

Vestibular diagnostics to identify and address dizziness and balance disorders.

Why Choose Us

Hearing Care Done Right

We go beyond the basics to ensure every patient receives the best possible hearing outcome.

Real-Ear Measurements

We use probe microphone verification on every hearing aid fitting — the gold standard that only 30% of providers follow.

Personalized Care

Every treatment plan is customized to your lifestyle, hearing needs, and budget — never a one-size-fits-all approach.

Certified Lenire Provider

We offer FDA-approved Lenire tinnitus treatment — one of the few providers in the region with this certification.
