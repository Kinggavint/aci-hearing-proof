Our Services

Complete hearing care for every stage of your hearing journey.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Hearing Care Services

From initial evaluation to ongoing care, we provide a full range of audiology services for patients of all ages in the Lafayette and Acadiana area.

Hearing Tests & Evaluations

Comprehensive audiometric testing including speech audiometry, tympanometry, and auditory brainstem response (ABR) to determine the type and degree of hearing loss.

Learn more

Hearing Aid Fittings

Custom calibration of hearing aids using real-ear measurements — the gold standard in fitting verification. Pre-fitting consultations ensure the right device for your needs.

Learn more

Hearing Aids

We carry hearing aids from top brands including Widex, Starkey, Phonak, Oticon, ReSound, Signia, Unitron, Rexton, Lyric, and CapTel.

Learn more

Cochlear Implants

Evaluation and candidacy assessment for cochlear implants for adults and children, including Cochlear Nucleus devices.

Learn more

Tinnitus Management

Comprehensive tinnitus treatment including FDA-approved Lenire therapy, sound maskers, CBT, hearing aids, and tinnitus retraining therapy.

Learn more

Balance Testing

Vestibular diagnostics to evaluate and address dizziness, vertigo, and balance disorders. Comprehensive assessment of your vestibular system.

Hearing Aid Repair

Expert repair and maintenance services to keep your hearing aids performing at their best. Same-day service available for many repairs.

Hearing Protection

Custom-molded hearing protection for noise exposure at work, concerts, hunting, and other activities. Protect your hearing before damage occurs.
