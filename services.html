Expanded Services

Specialized Diagnostic & Custom Mold Services

Advanced testing and custom-crafted solutions tailored to your unique hearing needs, lifestyle, and hearing health goals.

Diagnostic Testing

ABR

Auditory Brainstem Response — Advanced diagnostic testing that measures auditory nerve and brainstem function. Useful for patients of all ages, including infants.

ASSR

Auditory Steady-State Response — Frequency-specific hearing threshold estimation for patients who cannot respond to traditional testing methods.

DPOAE

Distortion Product Otoacoustic Emissions — Objective test of outer hair cell function in the inner ear. Fast, non-invasive, and effective for all ages.

Custom Molds & Ear Protection

Musician Molds

Custom-fitted ear protection designed specifically for musicians to reduce volume while preserving sound clarity and full-spectrum fidelity.

Snore Molds

Custom oral appliances designed to help reduce snoring for a better night’s rest for you and your partner.

Swim Molds

Waterproof custom molds to protect your ears during swimming and water activities, preventing swimmer’s ear and infection.

Other Custom & Non-Custom Molds

A variety of specialized ear molds for different needs, including sleeping molds, hunting protection, and more. Ask our team for options.

Additional Hearing Solutions

Phone Serenity Choice

Assistive listening accessories for clear, comfortable phone conversations — ideal for patients with hearing loss who struggle on calls.

Custom Ear Buds

Personalized ear buds molded to your unique ear shape for exceptional comfort, superior sound quality, and a secure fit.

OTC Hearing Aids

Over-the-Counter Hearing Aids — Guidance and professional support for over-the-counter hearing aid options to help you navigate the growing OTC market.
