Insurance and Payment Options

ACI Hearing Center accepts most major insurance plans for hearing evaluations and offers flexible payment options for hearing aids. We submit claims for you and verify your benefits before your appointment so there are no surprises.

Insurance plans we accept

ACI Hearing Center accepts most major insurance plans for hearing evaluations. Coverage for hearing aids varies by plan and policy year. We are in-network with:

  • Blue Cross Blue Shield of Louisiana
  • Medicare (Parts A and B for diagnostic testing)
  • Medicare Advantage plans with hearing benefits
  • Louisiana Medicaid (pediatric coverage)
  • United Healthcare
  • Aetna
  • Cigna
  • Humana
  • Tricare (active duty and retired military)

Do not see your plan? Call us anyway at (337) 223-9448 — we work with most insurers and will verify your specific benefits before your appointment.

Medicare and hearing aids — what you need to know

This is the most common insurance question we get. Here is the plain answer:

Traditional Medicare (Parts A and B): Does NOT cover hearing aids or routine hearing exams. It WILL cover a diagnostic hearing test if your physician orders one for a medical reason (sudden hearing loss, dizziness, ear pain, etc.).

Medicare Advantage (Part C): Many Advantage plans include hearing aid benefits. Coverage and allowance amounts vary by plan and year. Bring your Advantage plan card and we will verify your specific benefit before you commit to anything.

Financing and payment options

Hearing aids are a significant investment, and we understand not everyone can pay all at once. We offer:

  • CareCredit — 0% interest financing for qualified applicants, 6 to 24 month terms. Apply online or in our office.
  • Cherry — 0% interest plans with a soft-pull prequalification option.
  • PowerPay — Third-party installment financing, strong approval rates.
  • Manufacturer financing — Some hearing aid brands offer their own payment plans through partner programs.
  • Trade-in credit — Upgrading older hearing aids? We offer trade-in value toward your new pair.
  • Self-pay — We accept cash, all major credit cards, and HSA/FSA cards.

See our full hearing aid financing page for details on CareCredit, Cherry, PowerPay, and manufacturer payment programs.

What we file for you

We submit claims directly to your insurance company on your behalf. You pay only your portion — copay, coinsurance, or deductible — at the time of service. If your insurance denies a claim, our billing team helps you appeal. You should not have to fight the insurance battle alone.

Veterans benefits

The Department of Veterans Affairs provides hearing aids at no cost to eligible veterans through VA medical facilities. If you are a veteran, you may be able to receive hearing services through the VA Lafayette Outpatient Clinic. ACI Hearing Center also serves veterans through private pay or community care referrals when appropriate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What insurance plans does ACI Hearing Center accept?

We accept most major insurance plans for hearing evaluations including Blue Cross Blue Shield of Louisiana, Medicare, Medicaid, United Healthcare, Aetna, Cigna, Humana, and Tricare. Coverage for hearing aids varies by plan. Call (337) 223-9448 with your insurance card and we will verify your specific benefits.

Does Medicare cover hearing aids?

Traditional Medicare (Parts A and B) does NOT cover hearing aids or routine hearing exams. It does cover diagnostic hearing tests when ordered by a physician for a medical reason. Many Medicare Advantage (Part C) plans include hearing aid benefits — coverage varies by plan and year. We will check your specific plan.

Does Louisiana Medicaid cover hearing aids?

Louisiana Medicaid covers hearing aids for children up to age 21 through the EPSDT (Early and Periodic Screening, Diagnostic, and Treatment) benefit. Adult coverage is limited. Call us with your Medicaid card and we will confirm your specific benefits.

Do you accept Blue Cross Blue Shield of Louisiana?

Yes. We are in-network with BCBS of Louisiana for hearing evaluations. Hearing aid coverage depends on your specific plan. We will verify benefits before your appointment so there are no surprises.

What if my insurance does not cover hearing aids?

We offer flexible payment options including CareCredit financing with 0% interest plans, manufacturer payment programs, and trade-in credit for older hearing aids. We work with every family to find a path that fits the budget. Hearing aids range widely in price depending on the level of technology you choose.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. We accept CareCredit, which offers 0% interest financing for qualified buyers on plans from 6 to 24 months. We also work directly with families on manufacturer-financed options when needed.

Will you submit insurance claims for me?

Yes. We submit claims directly to your insurance company for you. You pay only your portion (deductible, copay, coinsurance) at the time of service. If your insurance denies a claim we will help you appeal.

How much does a hearing test cost?

Diagnostic hearing evaluations are typically covered by insurance when medically necessary. Self-pay rates are competitive — call (337) 223-9448 for current pricing. Most patients pay only their copay or deductible.

Does the VA cover hearing aids?

Yes, the Department of Veterans Affairs provides hearing aids at no cost to eligible veterans through VA medical facilities. ACI Hearing Center can provide private-pay hearing aid services for veterans who prefer to use private providers or who supplement VA care.
