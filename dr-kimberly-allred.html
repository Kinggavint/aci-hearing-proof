About Dr. Kimberly Allred

Dr. Kimberly (Peezie) L. Allred, Au.D., CCC-A, FAAA, is the owner of ACI Hearing Center in Lafayette, Louisiana. She has led the practice for over two decades and has been in audiology since 1994.

Dr. Allred graduated from the University of Southwestern Louisiana in 1994 with her Masters Degree in Audiology. In 2002, she graduated with her Doctor of Audiology degree from the University of Florida.

Dr. Allred is deeply committed to providing comprehensive, patient-centered care focused on improving each patient's overall quality of life. With extensive clinical experience in the diagnosis and treatment of hearing disorders across the lifespan, she is known for delivering thorough evaluations and personalized solutions tailored to each individual's needs.

Dr. Allred's expertise includes transforming lives through advanced hearing technology, including hearing aids and cochlear implants. She has specialized experience in cochlear implant services and tinnitus management and is a certified provider of the Lenire Tinnitus Treatment System, offering advanced, evidence-based treatment options for patients seeking relief from bothersome tinnitus. Her approach is rooted in best practices, education, and compassionate care, ensuring every patient feels heard, understood, and confident in their treatment plan.

Outside of the clinic, Dr. Allred is a devoted wife and mother of two. She enjoys spending quality time with her family, traveling, and embracing the outdoors, especially at the beach or out on the water fishing.

Areas of specialty

  • Comprehensive diagnostic hearing evaluations for all ages
  • Hearing aid fitting, programming, and long-term follow-up
  • Cochlear implant candidacy evaluation and mapping
  • Tinnitus management, including certified Lenire Tinnitus Treatment provider
  • Real-ear measurement verified hearing aid fittings
  • Pediatric audiology across the lifespan

Education and training

Dr. Allred completed her Masters Degree in Audiology at the University of Southwestern Louisiana in 1994 and her Doctor of Audiology (Au.D.) degree at the University of Florida in 2002. She continues to pursue ongoing continuing education in cochlear implant mapping, tinnitus management (including Lenire), and the latest hearing aid technology. She holds her Certificate of Clinical Competence in Audiology (CCC-A) through the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association and is a Fellow of the American Academy of Audiology (FAAA).

Frequently asked questions

How long has Dr. Kimberly Allred been practicing audiology?

Dr. Kimberly Allred has been practicing audiology since 1994, when she earned her Masters degree from the University of Southwestern Louisiana. She earned her Doctor of Audiology from the University of Florida in 2002. She has cared for patients in Lafayette Parish for over two decades and is the owner of ACI Hearing Center.

What are Dr. Allred's specialties?

Dr. Allred specializes in hearing aids, cochlear implants, tinnitus management, and comprehensive diagnostic hearing evaluations. She is a certified provider of the Lenire Tinnitus Treatment System and has extensive experience with patients of all ages, including pediatric and adult cochlear implant recipients.

Does Dr. Allred see pediatric patients?

Yes. Dr. Allred sees patients of all ages, from infants and toddlers through adults and seniors. She uses age-appropriate testing methods for children.

How do I schedule an appointment specifically with Dr. Allred?

Call ACI Hearing Center at 337-223-9448 and let our team know you would like to see Dr. Allred. We will do our best to schedule you at a time that works for both of you. New patient appointments typically take 60 to 90 minutes.

Is Dr. Allred board-certified in audiology?

Yes. Dr. Allred holds her Certificate of Clinical Competence in Audiology (CCC-A) through the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association, is a Fellow of the American Academy of Audiology (FAAA), and is licensed by the Louisiana Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology.

Which insurance plans does Dr. Allred accept?

Dr. Allred and ACI Hearing Center accept most major Louisiana insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield of Louisiana, Medicare, and many others. Coverage varies by plan. Call the office at 337-223-9448 to verify your specific benefits before your appointment.
