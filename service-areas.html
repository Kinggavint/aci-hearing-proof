Our audiology service areas across Acadiana

ACI Hearing Center has served Lafayette Parish and the surrounding Acadiana region since 1984. Our four Doctors of Audiology see patients from eight named communities: Broussard, Youngsville, Carencro, Scott, Maurice, Duson, Breaux Bridge, and Crowley. Every visit takes place at our Lafayette office at 103 Saint Thomas Street.

Call 337-223-9448

How to use this page

This is a hub for patients across Acadiana. Two communities have full-length dedicated pages: Broussard and Lafayette. The six sections below cover Youngsville, Carencro, Scott, Maurice, Duson, Breaux Bridge, and Crowley with drive times, service notes, and quick answers. Every ACI patient is seen by our team of Doctors of Audiology in Lafayette regardless of home town.

Audiology care for Broussard patients

Broussard patients reach our Lafayette office in about 10 minutes via U.S. 90 north or Pinhook Road. Our full Audiologist Serving Broussard, LA page covers driving directions, service scope, and the questions Broussard families ask most often. Book a diagnostic hearing test, hearing aid fitting, tinnitus consult, or cochlear implant candidacy visit at 337-223-9448.

Full Broussard page

Audiology care for Youngsville patients

Youngsville sits directly south of Lafayette, roughly 12 minutes from 103 Saint Thomas Street via LA 89 and Verot School Road. Youngsville families come to ACI Hearing Center for pediatric hearing tests, adult diagnostic evaluations, hearing aid fittings from Oticon, Phonak, ReSound, Signia, Starkey, and Widex, and tinnitus care including the Lenire device. Our Doctors of Audiology handle cochlear implant candidacy and lifetime device mapping so Youngsville patients no longer need to travel to New Orleans or Houston for CI follow-up. Call 337-223-9448 to schedule.

Audiology care for Carencro patients

Carencro sits north of Lafayette off I-49, about 12 minutes from our office. Carencro patients regularly visit ACI for diagnostic audiologic evaluations, hearing aid fittings, custom hearing protection for hunters and musicians, and tinnitus care. Our four Doctors of Audiology see all Carencro patients in Lafayette and coordinate follow-up care so you keep the same provider from your first hearing test through years of hearing aid support. Call 337-223-9448 for a same-week appointment.

Audiology care for Scott patients

Scott is just west of Lafayette along I-10, about 10 minutes from our office. Scott patients come to ACI for hearing tests, hearing aid fittings and repairs, cochlear implant mapping, and diagnostic testing for balance concerns. Because all appointments happen at 103 Saint Thomas Street, Scott families benefit from the same Doctor of Audiology continuity every visit. Call 337-223-9448 to book.

Audiology care for Maurice patients

Maurice sits south of Lafayette off LA 167 in Vermilion Parish, roughly 20 minutes from our office. Maurice patients drive to ACI for comprehensive audiologic evaluations, hearing aid consultations, tinnitus assessment, and custom hearing protection. Our Doctors of Audiology see Maurice families for full-course care from pediatric hearing screenings through adult diagnostic workups and long-term hearing aid follow-up. Call 337-223-9448 to schedule.

Audiology care for Duson patients

Duson is west of Lafayette along I-10, about 15 minutes from our Lafayette office. Duson patients visit ACI for diagnostic hearing tests, hearing aid fittings, hearing aid repairs, and tinnitus care. Our Doctors of Audiology coordinate cochlear implant candidacy and mapping for Duson patients who need that level of care, eliminating the need to travel to major metros. Call 337-223-9448 to book a visit.

Audiology care for Breaux Bridge patients

Breaux Bridge is east of Lafayette off I-10 in St. Martin Parish, roughly 15 minutes from our office. Breaux Bridge patients come to ACI for diagnostic audiology evaluations, hearing aid fittings, tinnitus care, and custom hearing protection. Because our four Doctors of Audiology all see patients in one Lafayette office, Breaux Bridge families keep consistent provider relationships across years of care. Call 337-223-9448 to schedule.

Audiology care for Crowley patients

Crowley is west of Lafayette along I-10 in Acadia Parish, roughly 25 minutes from our office. Crowley patients travel to ACI for diagnostic hearing tests, hearing aid consultations, cochlear implant mapping, and tinnitus care including the Lenire device. Our Doctors of Audiology serve Crowley families with the same standard of care our Lafayette patients receive. Call 337-223-9448 to book.

Coming from outside these eight communities?

Patients also visit us from New Iberia, Opelousas, Abbeville, Eunice, and other Acadiana towns. If you are farther out, call and we will help you plan the visit and combine your diagnostic evaluation with a fitting or follow-up on the same day. Call 337-223-9448.
