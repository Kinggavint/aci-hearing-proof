Audiology Care for Lafayette Residents

ACI Hearing Center has been the trusted audiology practice in Lafayette, Louisiana, since 1984. Our office at 103 Saint Thomas Street sits in the heart of Lafayette, a short drive from every major Lafayette neighborhood and from the surrounding Acadiana communities. We are the choice of patients who want medical-model audiology from Doctors of Audiology, not retail hearing aid sales.

We serve every Lafayette resident from newborns through retirement age. Pediatric hearing evaluations, adult diagnostic testing, hearing aid fittings, tinnitus treatment, and cochlear implant candidacy and mapping are all handled in-house. Patients drive in from across Lafayette Parish and from neighboring parishes including Acadia, Iberia, St. Martin, St. Landry, and Vermilion.

Services Available to Lafayette Patients

Every audiology service is delivered in our Lafayette office. Patients do not have to drive to Baton Rouge, New Orleans, or Houston for cochlear implant mapping, Lenire-certified tinnitus treatment, or pediatric audiology — all of it is available here in Lafayette.

  • Comprehensive Hearing Tests — full diagnostic audiogram, speech-in-noise testing, tympanometry, otoacoustic emissions.
  • Hearing Aid Fitting — Oticon, Phonak, ReSound, Starkey, Widex, Signia, Unitron, Lyric.
  • Tinnitus Management — certified Lenire provider, sound therapy, TRT, CBT referral.
  • Cochlear Implant Evaluation and Mapping — candidacy testing, surgical referral, lifetime mapping.
  • Diagnostic Testing — ABR, ECochG, otoacoustic emissions, and comprehensive audiometry.
  • Custom Ear Molds — swim plugs, musician plugs, sleep plugs, hunter and shooter protection.
  • Hearing Protection — custom and over-the-counter solutions for work, music, and recreation.
  • Hearing Aid Repair — in-house repair on most major brands regardless of where the device was purchased.

Conditions We Treat for Lafayette Patients

  • Sensorineural hearing loss (age-related, noise-induced, sudden)
  • Conductive hearing loss
  • Mixed hearing loss
  • Tinnitus (chronic ringing, buzzing, hissing, or pulsatile)
  • Auditory processing disorders
  • Cerumen (earwax) impaction
  • Single-sided deafness
  • Sudden sensorineural hearing loss (SSNHL)
  • Acoustic trauma and noise damage
  • Pediatric hearing concerns

Frequently Asked Questions — Lafayette Patients

Where is ACI Hearing Center located in Lafayette, LA?

ACI Hearing Center is at 103 Saint Thomas Street in Lafayette, Louisiana, 70506. The office is in central Lafayette between downtown and the Oil Center, with free on-site parking and full wheelchair access.

Do I need a referral from my primary care doctor to see an audiologist in Lafayette?

No referral is required for an audiology evaluation at ACI Hearing Center. Patients can self-refer by calling 337-223-9448 or by using the contact form on our site. Some insurance plans require a referral for coverage of certain tests — we verify that for you before your visit.

How much does a hearing test cost in Lafayette, LA?

Diagnostic hearing tests at ACI Hearing Center are typically covered by Medicare, Medicaid, and most commercial insurance. For self-pay patients the comprehensive hearing evaluation is priced transparently and quoted before your visit. Call 337-223-9448 for your specific coverage.

Which hearing aid brands does ACI Hearing Center fit in Lafayette?

ACI fits every major hearing aid brand — Oticon, Phonak, ReSound, Starkey, Widex, Signia, Unitron, and Lyric extended-wear devices. We are brand-independent, so the recommendation is matched to your hearing loss and lifestyle rather than to a single manufacturer.

Does ACI Hearing Center treat tinnitus in Lafayette?

Yes. ACI is the certified Lenire provider in Lafayette and Acadiana for FDA-approved bimodal neuromodulation tinnitus treatment. We also offer sound therapy, tinnitus retraining therapy, hearing aid fittings with built-in tinnitus maskers, and referral coordination for cognitive behavioral therapy.

Can I get a cochlear implant evaluation in Lafayette?

Yes. ACI Hearing Center provides cochlear implant candidacy evaluation, pre-surgical workup, surgical referral, and lifetime device mapping in Lafayette. Patients do not need to travel to New Orleans, Baton Rouge, or Houston for cochlear implant audiology care.

How long does a hearing test appointment take at ACI?

A comprehensive diagnostic audiologic evaluation takes 60 to 90 minutes for new patients. This includes full case history, otoscopy, tympanometry, pure-tone audiometry, speech-in-noise testing, otoacoustic emissions, and a results review with your audiologist.

How quickly can I get an audiology appointment in Lafayette?

Most Lafayette patients are seen within 3 to 5 business days of calling. Call 337-223-9448 to schedule. We hold same-day slots for urgent concerns like sudden hearing loss.

Ready to schedule your hearing evaluation?

Lafayette patients can call, text, or message us to book a hearing test or follow-up visit.
