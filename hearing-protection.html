Hearing Protection

Custom-fitted protection to preserve the hearing you have — before noise exposure causes permanent damage.

Protect Your Hearing in Acadiana

Noise-induced hearing loss is 100% preventable — yet it is one of the most common and permanent forms of hearing damage. Lafayette and the surrounding Acadiana region have significant industries and recreational activities that can pose real risks to your hearing: oil and gas operations, manufacturing, hunting season, motorsports, and live music are all part of daily life here.

ACI Hearing Center offers professional, custom-fitted hearing protection solutions that go far beyond what you can find at a hardware store. Custom earplugs are made from an impression of your ear canal, providing a precise fit that is more comfortable, more effective, and more durable than disposable foam plugs.

Why Custom Beats Over-the-Counter

Over-the-Counter Earplugs

  • One-size-fits-most — often fits poorly
  • May be difficult to insert correctly
  • Inconsistent noise reduction
  • Uncomfortable for extended wear
  • Disposable and add up in cost over time
  • Limited options for specific applications

Custom-Molded Protection

  • Made from an impression of your exact ear canal
  • Superior seal and consistent noise reduction
  • Comfortable for all-day wear
  • Durable — last for years with proper care
  • Available with specialty filters for different uses
  • Hygienic and easy to clean

Types of Custom Hearing Protection We Offer

Occupational & Industrial Protection

Workers in oil and gas, manufacturing, construction, and other noisy industries face daily noise exposure that can accumulate over a career. Custom earplugs provide the reliable, consistent protection needed for an 8-hour shift.

Musician's Earplugs

Musicians need protection that reduces volume without distorting the sound they need to hear. Musician's earplugs use acoustic filters to reduce sound levels evenly across all frequencies, preserving the full fidelity of music.

Hunting & Shooting Protection

Firearms produce some of the highest impulse noise levels possible — a single gunshot can permanently damage hearing. Custom hunting plugs and electronic hearing protection allow you to hear conversation and environmental sounds normally while blocking the damaging impulse of each shot.

Motorsports & Recreational

Motorcycles, ATVs, racing, and power sports generate sustained high noise levels. Custom ear protection is designed to fit comfortably under helmets and reduce wind and engine noise during long rides.

Swim Molds

Custom swim plugs keep water out of the ear canal, making them ideal for swimmers, surfers, and anyone prone to swimmer's ear or with a perforated eardrum. Comfortable, floatable, and available in bright colors.

Sleep Plugs

Custom sleep plugs are made with soft, flexible material for all-night comfort. They provide consistent, reliable sound reduction for light sleepers, shift workers, or anyone sharing a sleep environment with noise.

The Custom Fitting Process

Getting custom hearing protection at ACI Hearing Center is simple:

  1. Consultation — We discuss your specific noise exposure, activities, and goals for protection.
  2. Ear impressions — We take comfortable silicone impressions of your ear canals. The process takes only a few minutes.
  3. Laboratory fabrication — Your impressions are sent to a laboratory where your custom earplugs are precision-crafted to your exact ear shape.
  4. Fitting & delivery — When your earplugs arrive, we fit them and instruct you on proper insertion, care, and maintenance.

Noise-Induced Hearing Loss Is Permanent

Unlike some medical conditions, noise-induced hearing loss cannot be reversed or restored. The hair cells in the inner ear that are destroyed by noise exposure do not grow back. That makes prevention the only real solution.

If you work in a noisy environment, enjoy loud recreational activities, or simply want to protect the hearing you have, contact ACI Hearing Center today. The investment in custom protection is small compared to the lifelong cost of hearing loss.
