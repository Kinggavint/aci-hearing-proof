About ACI Hearing Center

42 years of trusted hearing care in Lafayette and the Acadiana region.

Our Story

Serving Lafayette Since 1984

For over 42 years, ACI Hearing Center has been a trusted name in hearing healthcare throughout Lafayette and the Acadiana region. Founded in 1984, our practice has grown from a small clinic into one of the area's most respected audiology practices.

What has remained constant through the decades is our commitment to providing the highest quality care. We believe that better hearing means a better quality of life, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality for each and every patient.

The ACI Hearing Center team — Lafayette, Louisiana

Why Patients Choose ACI
