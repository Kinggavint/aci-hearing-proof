Meet Our Team

The experienced audiologists and staff behind your better hearing.

Our Audiologists & Staff

Experienced, Caring Professionals

Our team of four Doctors of Audiology and dedicated support staff is committed to helping you hear your best.

Dr. Kimberly L. Allred

Au.D., CCC-A, FAAA — Doctor of Audiology / Practice Owner

Dr. Kimberly (Peezie) L. Allred graduated from the University of Southwestern Louisiana in 1994 with her Masters Degree in Audiology. In 2002, she graduated with her Doctor of Audiology degree from the University of Florida.

Dr. Allred is deeply committed to providing comprehensive, patient-centered care focused on improving each patient’s overall quality of life. With extensive clinical experience in the diagnosis and treatment of hearing disorders across the lifespan, she is known for delivering thorough evaluations and personalized solutions tailored to each individual’s needs.

Dr. Allred’s expertise includes transforming lives through advanced hearing technology, including hearing aids and cochlear implants. She has specialized experience in cochlear implant services and tinnitus management and is a certified provider of the Lenire Tinnitus Treatment System, offering advanced, evidence-based treatment options for patients seeking relief from bothersome tinnitus. Her approach is rooted in best practices, education, and compassionate care—ensuring every patient feels heard, understood, and confident in their treatment plan.

Outside of the clinic, Dr. Allred is a devoted wife and mother of two. She enjoys spending quality time with her family, traveling, and embracing the outdoors—especially at the beach or out on the water fishing.

Dr. Elizabeth LeMaire

Au.D. — Doctor of Audiology

Dr. Elizabeth LeMaire is a Doctor of Audiology based in Louisiana, originally from Zachary with family roots in Abbeville. She earned her Bachelor of Arts in Communication Disorders from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette in 2012 and her Doctorate in Audiology from LSU Health Sciences Center in New Orleans in 2017. Prior to joining ACI Hearing Center in 2017, she completed her fourth-year externship at the New Orleans VA Hospital.

Dr. LeMaire is committed to providing comprehensive, patient-centered care focused on improving overall quality of life. She has extensive clinical experience in the diagnosis and treatment of hearing disorders across the lifespan, with specialized expertise in cochlear implant and tinnitus management. Additionally, she is a certified provider of the Lenire Tinnitus Treatment System.

By day, she is dedicated to helping patients achieve meaningful improvements in communication and hearing function. Outside of her clinical practice, she is a devoted wife and mother to two daughters, who inspire her daily to bring patience, attentiveness, and compassion into all aspects of her work.

Outside the clinic, she values time with her family and enjoys creating meaningful moments with her husband and their two daughters.

Dr. Sarah Lalande-Davies

Au.D. — Doctor of Audiology

Dr. Sarah Lalande Davies joined our practice in November 2020. A Baton Rouge native, she earned her Bachelor of Science in Communication Sciences and Disorders from Louisiana State University and her Doctor of Audiology (Au.D.) from LSU Health Sciences Center in New Orleans. She completed her fourth-year externship at the Southeast Louisiana Veterans Health Care System, where she had the honor of serving our nation’s veterans. She is passionate about helping patients reconnect with the sounds that matter most, finds great joy in seeing the impact improved hearing has on their daily lives, and enjoys getting to know each patient along the way.

When she’s not in the clinic, Dr. Davies enjoys spending time with her husband, son, and dog, especially going for walks together and visiting the local farmers market. She and her family are LSU football season ticket holders and love cheering on the Tigers. She also enjoys traveling, reading, listening to music, and spending time with family and friends.

Dr. Courtney Barbier

Au.D. — Doctor of Audiology

Dr. Courtney Barbier is a native of Lafayette, LA. She received her Bachelor of Arts in Speech Pathology & Audiology from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette in 2018 and then went on to receive her Doctorate in Audiology from Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center in New Orleans in 2022. Prior to graduating, Dr. Barbier completed her fourth-year externship at ACI Hearing & Balance Center. She has worked in a variety of clinical settings, including ENT clinics and hospitals, where she provided comprehensive audiological care. Dr. Barbier is licensed by the Louisiana Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology and holds her Certificate of Clinical Competence in Audiology through the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association. She is an active member of the American Academy of Audiology and the Louisiana Academy of Audiology. Outside of work, Dr. Barbier enjoys spending time with her family and friends, watching sports, and traveling.

Keri Stevens

Audiology Assistant

Keri serves as an Audiology Assistant here at ACI Hearing Center, where she specializes in providing compassionate patient support and ensuring a seamless clinical experience. Her dedication to high-quality care is driven by a passion for helping others live fuller, more connected lives through better hearing. Outside of her professional role, Keri is a family-oriented individual who shares her home with several cherished pets. She is also a creative spirit who enjoys the art of cooking and various crafting hobbies.

Tina Ray

Patient Care Coordinator

Tina earned her Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Southwestern Louisiana in 1992 and brings more than 25 years of experience in the hearing healthcare field. Since joining our practice in 2017, she has been a key part of the team as a Patient Care Coordinator—keeping schedules on track, navigating insurance and finances, and making sure patients feel comfortable and cared for from the moment they arrive. What she enjoys most is building relationships with patients and seeing the real difference better hearing can make in their everyday lives. Outside the office, Tina is married and the proud mom of two adult daughters. She loves staying connected with her friends—whether that’s over a great meal, on a fun trip, or just enjoying time together. When she’s not out and about, you’ll likely find her reading with her book club or enjoying her love of musical theater.
