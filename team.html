Our Audiologists & Staff

Experienced, Caring Professionals

Our team of four Doctors of Audiology and dedicated support staff is committed to helping you hear your best.

Dr. Kimberly L. Allred

Au.D., CCC-A, FAAA — Doctor of Audiology / Practice Owner

Dr. Kimberly (Peezie) L. Allred graduated from the University of Southwestern Louisiana in 1994 with her Masters Degree in Audiology. In 2002, she graduated with her Doctor of Audiology degree from the University of Florida. She began working for ACI Hearing & Balance Center, Inc. in 1994 as both an Audiologist and Office Manager. In January 2002, she purchased the practice from Dr. Henry Trahan, Au.D. Dr. Allred specializes in diagnostic audiology, including infant to adult hearing evaluations, custom molds (including musician’s molds, hearing protection, and swim molds), vestibular (balance) testing, Auditory Brainstem Response Testing (ABR), Distortion Product Otoacoustic Emission Testing (DPOAE), hearing aid selections, fittings and programming of the latest in Digital Technology.

Dr. Elizabeth LeMaire

Au.D., FAAA — Doctor of Audiology

Dr. Elizabeth LeMaire is a native of Zachary, LA with family roots in Abbeville, LA. She received her Bachelors of Arts degree in Communication Disorders from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette in 2012 and then went on to receive her Doctorate in Audiology from Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center in New Orleans in May 2017. She is a fellow of the American Academy of Audiology and is a member of Louisiana Academy of Audiology. Her interest in audiology lies in the diagnosis and treatment of hearing and vestibular disorders, tinnitus management, and amplification for children and the aging population. Prior to joining ACI Hearing and Balance Center, she completed her fourth year externship at the New Orleans VA Hospital where she worked with veterans of all ages. Elizabeth and her husband, Matt, enjoy travelling and exploring new places, entertaining friends and family, and being active.

Dr. Sarah Lalande-Davies

Au.D. — Doctor of Audiology

Dr. Sarah Lalande-Davies joined our practice at the end of November 2020. She is a native of Baton Rouge, Louisiana. Born and raised a Tiger fan, she graduated with a Bachelor’s in Communication Sciences and Disorders from Louisiana State University and holds a Doctorate in Audiology from Louisiana State University Health and Sciences Center New Orleans. She completed her fourth-year externship serving our nation’s veterans at the Southeast Louisiana Veterans Health Care System. Dr. Lalande-Davies is a licensed audiologist and carries the Certificate of Clinical Competence in Audiology (CCC-A).

Dr. Courtney Barbier

Au.D. — Doctor of Audiology

Dr. Courtney Barbier is a native of Lafayette, LA. She received her Bachelor of Arts in Speech Pathology & Audiology from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette in 2018 and then went on to receive her Doctorate in Audiology from Louisiana State University Health Sciences Center in New Orleans in 2022. Prior to graduating, Dr. Barbier completed her fourth-year externship at ACI Hearing & Balance Center. She has worked in a variety of clinical settings, including ENT clinics and hospitals, where she provided comprehensive audiological care. Dr. Barbier is licensed by the Louisiana Board of Examiners for Speech-Language Pathology and Audiology and holds her Certificate of Clinical Competence in Audiology through the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association. She is an active member of the American Academy of Audiology and the Louisiana Academy of Audiology. Outside of work, Dr. Barbier enjoys spending time with her family and friends, watching sports, and traveling.

Keri Stevens

Audiology Assistant

Keri serves as an Audiology Assistant here at ACI Hearing Center, where she specializes in providing compassionate patient support and ensuring a seamless clinical experience. Her dedication to high-quality care is driven by a passion for helping others live fuller, more connected lives through better hearing. Outside of her professional role, Keri is a family-oriented individual who shares her home with several cherished pets. She is also a creative spirit who enjoys the art of cooking and various crafting hobbies.

Tina Ray

Patient Care Coordinator

Tina graduated with her Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette in 1992. She has spent the last 17 years in the Hearing Healthcare Industry and joined our practice in 2017. As a patient care coordinator, her duties include scheduling, insurance claims, general patient care and accounts payable and receivable.
