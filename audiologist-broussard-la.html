Services Available to Broussard Patients

Every audiology service Broussard patients need is available at our Lafayette office — no long-distance trips required. Broussard patients who previously drove to Houston or New Orleans for cochlear implant mapping, Lenire-certified tinnitus care, or pediatric audiology can now have it done locally.

  • Hearing Aid Repair — in-house repair on most major brands regardless of where the device was purchased.

How far is ACI Hearing Center from Broussard, LA?
