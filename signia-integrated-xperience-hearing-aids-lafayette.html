Why patients pick Signia IX over other brands

Almost every hearing aid manufacturer says they are good in noise. Only Signia has RealTime Conversation Enhancement (RTCE). RTCE identifies up to three separate voices around you, tracks each one, and can enhance each one individually. When your grandkids are all talking at once, or you are at a table of four at a restaurant on Kaliste Saloom, or you are the one moderating a small meeting, RTCE is doing something the other brands cannot match. This is Signia's defining feature, and the reason group listening is what Signia does best.

The second reason: the Silk Charge and Go IX. Most hearing aids that are invisible-in-canal require custom earmold impressions and a two week wait. Silk IX ships as a ready-to-wear device that fits deep in the canal on Day 1, and it is fully rechargeable. If keeping the device hidden matters to you, this is the Signia answer.

Signia IX models we fit

Pure Charge and Go IX (flagship)

Own Voice Processing: solving the number-one first-time complaint

The most common complaint from first-time hearing aid wearers is that their own voice sounds boomy, hollow, or like they are talking in a barrel. That is a real acoustic phenomenon called occlusion, and it drives a lot of people to give up on their hearing aids in the first 30 days.

Signia has a feature called Own Voice Processing that learns the acoustic signature of your voice during the fitting. Once the hearing aid recognizes your voice, it processes your voice through a separate pathway from every other sound. The result: your own voice sounds natural while the world still sounds louder and clearer. This is one reason Signia has such a strong track record with patients transitioning from no hearing aids to their first pair.

Common questions about Signia IX

What makes Signia IX different from other hearing aids?

The RealTime Conversation Enhancement feature. When you are in a group of people talking, Signia IX can track up to three different voices at the same time and enhance each one individually. Other brands make the whole group louder. Signia decides which specific voice you are trying to follow and gives that voice a boost. This is the defining Signia feature and the reason group listening is what Signia IX does best.

What models are on the Signia IX platform?

Three main styles fit by ACI Hearing Center: Pure Charge and Go IX (behind-the-ear rechargeable receiver-in-canal), Silk Charge and Go IX (invisible-in-canal rechargeable with no earmold impressions), and Insio Charge and Go IX (custom in-the-ear rechargeable). We match the model to your ear shape, hearing loss, and lifestyle.

Is Silk Charge and Go IX really invisible?

Yes. Silk Charge and Go IX sits deep in the ear canal and is not visible from the side. It is the first fully rechargeable ready-to-wear completely-in-canal device that does not require earmold impressions. Most patients qualify for it if their hearing loss is mild to moderately severe.

What does a Signia treatment plan cost at ACI Hearing Center?

Our treatment plans at ACI Hearing Center range from $4,500 to $7,500 and include binaural hearing aids (both ears), a real-ear measurement fitting, follow-up adjustments, cleanings, and our care commitment for the life of your devices. Where you land in that range depends on the technology level and lifestyle needs, not the brand.

Does Signia IX work with an iPhone?

Yes. Signia IX streams directly from iPhone and from modern Android phones using Bluetooth LE Audio and Auracast. The Signia app on your phone gives you volume control, program switching, and access to the RealTime Conversation Enhancement feature.

What is Own Voice Processing and why does it matter?

Own Voice Processing (OVP) is a Signia feature that learns your unique voice signature during the fitting. Once it knows your voice, the hearing aid processes your own voice separately from other sounds. This reduces the boomy, echoey feeling many first-time hearing aid wearers describe when they hear themselves. It is one of the most-requested Signia features.

Can Signia IX help in noisy restaurants?

Yes. Restaurants and group settings are exactly where the RealTime Conversation Enhancement feature earns its keep. It identifies who is talking around you, tracks them, and enhances the voices you are following. Signia IX is the brand we most often recommend for patients whose top complaint is "I cannot follow group conversation in a noisy room."

How long does the battery last on a charge?

The Pure Charge and Go IX runs about 20 hours per charge with typical use. Silk Charge and Go IX runs about 24 hours. Streaming reduces runtime. The charger includes a portable case that stores extra charges for travel.
